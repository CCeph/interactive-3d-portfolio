About Me

I am a Biochemist turned Web Developer. Quite a jump, I know. While I loved studying about genetics in university and contributing to the research of life-saving drugs, recently I have found my passion in creating. Web development became that outlet for me. I also did not expect how fascinating design would be. Each piece of work now serves as a canvas with a story to be told through it. Additionally, I am quite thankful that I took my time at the beginning of my journey to gain solid foundations of HTML, CSS, and JS as it allows me to be as flexible and creative as possible with my work.
